Section 2 · 2:00 – 3:00 PM
Understanding the Role &
Creating a Transition Plan
Define what the role truly demands — accountabilities, excellence indicators, key relationships — then build a phased transition plan.
The 4 Essential Questions

Verifiable, specific, time-bound.

Include upward, lateral, external.

Phased Transition Plan
Phase 1 · Months 1–3
Shadow & Learn
Phase 2 · Months 3–6
Lead with Support
Phase 3 · Month 6+
Full Handover
Performance in current role — 2+ consecutive cycles above expectations
Competency Pattern — consistent behavior change, not one strong moment
Stakeholder Confidence — peers, direct reports & clients confirm genuinely
Stretch Assignment — delivered at a high level
Sponsor Sign-off — senior leader + 1 other confirm readiness genuinely
Section 3 · 3:00 – 4:00 PM
Values, GWC &
Competency Calibration
Rate independently first — do not share until everyone has finished. Then calibrate as a group to reach a consensus Readiness Tier.
+ = Consistent pattern  ·  +/– = Sometimes / Inconsistent  ·  – = Pattern of absence
Section 4 · 4:00 – 4:30 PM
Growth
Journey Map
One map per successor. Convert each Readiness Tier into a personalized development roadmap.
Ready Soon (2/3 GWC) → 0–3 and 3–6 month bands only.    Ready Later (1/3 GWC) → All four bands (0–18 months).
